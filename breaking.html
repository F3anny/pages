D-Wave's Quantum Leap: Outperforming Classical Supercomputers

D-Wave Quantum has announced a significant scientific breakthrough, demonstrating that its annealing quantum computer outperformed a top-tier classical supercomputer in solving complex magnetic material simulations.This achievement has led to a surge in quantum computing stocks, with D-Wave's stock rising over 11%, and other companies like IonQ and Rigetti Computing also seeing substantial gains. The technology shows promise for solving highly complex problems that surpass the capabilities of today's classical computers.

Firefly Aerospace's Historic Lunar Landing

On March 2, 2025, Firefly Aerospace successfully landed the Blue Ghost Mission 1 on the Moon as part of NASA's Commercial Lunar Payload Services program. The mission delivered payloads to Mare Crisium, including instruments to study lunar regoliths and the interactions between solar wind and Earth's magnetic field. This marks a significant milestone in commercial lunar exploration, showcasing the potential of private companies in advancing space science.

Colossal Biosciences' Woolly Mouse: A Step Towards De-Extinction

Colossal Biosciences has announced the creation of a "woolly mouse" with eight modified genes, expressing mammoth-like traits relevant to cold adaptation. This development serves as a platform for validating genome engineering targets and represents a significant step towards the de-extinction of the woolly mammoth. The breakthrough showcases the potential of genetic engineering in bringing back extinct species and adapting existing ones to changing environments.
